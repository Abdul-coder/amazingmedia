About Us

Located in Villupuram, Tamil Nadu, Amazing Multimedia offers hands-on training in Graphic Design, Animation, VFX, and Editing. Our aim is to foster creative talent through real-world seminars, live classes, and expert mentorship in a fun and professional environment.

Courses Offered

Graphic Design

Master Photoshop, Illustrator & design fundamentals with real-world projects.

2D/3D Animation

Learn character animation, rigging, and 3D modeling from industry experts.

Visual Effects (VFX)

Explore compositing, green screen effects, rotoscoping, and more.

Video Editing

Become skilled with Premiere Pro and After Effects for modern content creation.
